How it Works?


We are a start-up company, deeply passionate about both food and technology. We have joined forces to create the HomeMeals app, with the only mission of simplify and enhancing your home cooking experience.

Recipes with Step by Step videos


Discover your favorite breakfast, snack, lunch or dinner. We make cooking easy with a growing collection of video recipes perfect for everyone, no matter your kitchen experience



Learn to cook at home with us!

Discover how to Combine your favorite recipes or ingredients


Tired of repetitive meals? We have the solution, just choose one recipe you like and we'll show you dish combination ideas, or built your own combinations and visualize them in real time



Verify the nutritional values while you are combining!

Enjoy stress-free meal planning! We do the magic for you!


Save time with our meal planner assistant - we adjust your meals for up to 3 weeks, based on how often you cook each week. Or simply add your meal combinations into your meal planner to set up your meal plan



Track the nutritional values of your meal plans!

Smart grocery list built for you


With our grocery list you only buy what you need. Simply add your recipes, combinations or meal plans into your shopping list and define the servings you want to cook for. We’ll do the rest

Ready to cook for the week? We’ll calculate the ingredients you need, based on your servings and days you want to cook that meal for. Batch cooking or day by day cooking, both are great
